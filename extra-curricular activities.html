Extra-Curricular Activities

At International Delhi Public School (IDPS), we believe that a well-rounded education goes beyond academics, emphasizing the importance of extra-curricular activities that nurture students’ interests, talents, and social skills. Our extensive range of extra-curricular programs encourages students to explore their passions, build confidence, and develop essential life skills, contributing to their overall growth and well-being.

Key Areas of Extra-Curricular Activities at IDPS

Performing Arts (Music, Dance, and Drama)
IDPS offers students opportunities to participate in music, dance, and drama, allowing them to express themselves creatively. Music classes include training in both instrumental and vocal music, while dance classes cover various styles such as classical, contemporary, and traditional dances. Drama clubs and theater performances help students build confidence, improve public speaking, and develop teamwork. These activities culminate in annual performances, cultural festivals, and inter-school competitions, showcasing students’ talents.
Visual Arts and Craft
Art and craft classes provide students with a creative outlet to explore painting, sketching, sculpture, and crafts. Through guided sessions and workshops, students can express their creativity and develop skills in various artistic techniques. These activities help foster patience, concentration, and self-expression, contributing to students’ emotional and cognitive growth
Sports and Physical Education
Physical fitness and sportsmanship are key components of the IDPS experience. With facilities for basketball, football, cricket, badminton, table tennis, and more, students have multiple avenues to participate in sports activities. Regular coaching, inter-house and inter-school sports competitions, and physical fitness training allow students to develop discipline, teamwork, and resilience, all while staying physically fit.
Literary Activities and Public Speaking
For students with a flair for language and writing, IDPS offers opportunities in debating, storytelling, creative writing, and public speaking. Participation in these activities helps students improve their communication skills, think critically, and express themselves confidently. Debates, poetry recitations, and essay competitions are organized regularly to encourage intellectual engagement and expressive growth.
STEM Activities (Science, Robotics, and Space Exploration)
IDPS offers specialized programs focused on science, technology, engineering, and math (STEM) to ignite curiosity and a love for learning in these fields. Through hands-on projects in the robotics lab, science fairs, and space exploration sessions, students can experiment, innovate, and problem-solve in a collaborative environment. These activities foster scientific thinking and prepare students for the demands of a tech-driven future.
Language and Cultural Activities
At IDPS, language and cultural programs celebrate diversity and promote global awareness. Students have the opportunity to explore different languages, traditions, and cultural practices, fostering an appreciation for multiculturalism. Language activities and international exchange programs also encourage students to broaden their linguistic and cultural horizons.
Adventure and Field Trips
IDPS organizes regular field trips, excursions, and adventure camps that provide students with valuable learning experiences outside the classroom. These trips encourage independence, teamwork, and a love for nature, all while helping students learn in a practical and interactive way.
