About Us

International Delhi Public School

Welcome to International Delhi Public School (IDPS), we are dedicated to fostering an environment that nurtures academic excellence and holistic development. Our state-of-the-art campus is thoughtfully designed to create an inspiring, safe, and engaging learning environment for every student.

Situated in the verdant landscape of Cherukupalli, known for its rich cultural heritage and storied past, our school emerges as a beacon of modern education deeply rooted in timeless values.

Inaugurated in 2022 under the visionary guidance of Mr. PYK Reddy, our institution represents a harmonious blend of rural charm and global academic standards, emblematic of a new era in education.

Our sprawling campus, spread over five acres of lush greenery, stands as a silent testament to the beauty of the Coromandel Coast. It offers a tranquil oasis, far from the distractions of urban life, where students can immerse themselves in learning and self-discovery.

At the heart of our ethos lies a commitment to diversity and inclusivity. Our school proudly hosts a vibrant tapestry of students and educators from various backgrounds, fostering an environment where every culture, perspective, and individual is respected and valued.

About Us

Vision

At International Delhi Public School Cherukupalli, our vision is to cultivate a nurturing environment where academic excellence, holistic development, and global citizenship go hand in hand. We aim to empower students to become innovative thinkers, compassionate leaders, and responsible global citizens who are equipped to face the challenges of a rapidly changing world. Through a commitment to a progressive and inclusive education system, we aspire to inspire lifelong learning, foster critical thinking, and instill values that promote social responsibility, cultural understanding, and environmental sustainability

To empower every student to become a life-long learner who is responsible, productive, and an engaged citizen within the global community.

Beyond Academics

Physcology

What is meant by giving education? Is it just the transfer of knowledge from teachers to students? No. At International Delhi Public School, Cherukupalli, we believe true education happens when the child learns to think independently, learns to ask questions and even when he or she unlearns redundant knowledge. We inspire students to love the journey of learning through our teaching methods. Above all, your child will be attending classes with students coming from across the country. With such diversity to experience, your child’s world-view will expand.

Faculty

Each of our faculty members has a proven track record of educating students with a focus on building a strong fundamental knowledge and encouraging a zest for inquisitiveness among students. Our digital savvy teachers make full use of technology enabling interactive learning. Our teachers, with their academic plan, empower students to work and solve problems independently. At the same time, we focus on making students realise the value of collaboration.

Infrastructure

The school is where your child will spend a huge amount of time in his/her academic life. Hence, it is of utmost importance that the school infrastructure should facilitate the mental and physical growth of your child with no obstruction whatsoever. The infrastructure of International Delhi Public School is certified by the local authorities to be safe and free of hazards. The expansive, well-ventilated building makes sure that students can sit and play comfortably with enough leg room. We also have a decently large playground for students to enjoy their school-time. We have kept a special focus on sanitation and availability of clean drinking water. In short, the school matches the infrastructural standard of any global school.
