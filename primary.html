Primary School Education at International Delhi Public School

Primary education at International Delhi Public School (IDPS) lays a strong foundation for lifelong learning and personal growth. This stage is crucial as it shapes a child's cognitive, social, and emotional development. Our primary education program is thoughtfully designed to nurture curiosity, foster independence, and build essential skills in a supportive and engaging environment.
